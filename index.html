Profile

As a Process Improvement Workflow Analyst, my responsibility involves managing projects from their conception to their execution and beyond. My duties include overseeing the daily operations of processes, designing workflows, and ensuring their successful implementation. I possess significant expertise in the financial services industry.

Having more than 25 years of experience in workflow systems, I have acquired vast expertise in implementing and supporting Nintex/K2 Workflow solutions that seamlessly integrate with the FNZ platform. As a skilled workflow analyst, I have designed and implemented more than 250 complex business processes and played a significant role in designing crucial system functionality and UI design.

In addition, I have effectively overseen a team in integrating robotic process automation (RPA) into the workflow system, automating tasks and allowing users to concentrate on more intricate assignments. Throughout the entire process, from conception to delivery and ongoing support, I have exhibited expertise in designing and developing workflow systems.

Key skills and technical capabilities:

  • Working with business sponsors and SMEs in requirements gathering, scoping, system and process designing, prototyping, estimating, testing, and implementation of business process automation projects.
  • Experienced in engaging with the business, operational IT departments, and third-party providers as required to develop and support the workflow solutions
  • Implemented and chaired monthly workflow user group for all types of system users
  • Agile and Waterfall project life-cycle experience User experience (UX) / User interface (UI) design experience including storyboarding, prototyping, and producing wire-frame solution design
  • Strong analytical skills, with an ability to understand and communicate system-level impact to the Business and the user community
  • Experience in engaging key stakeholders in projects
  • Nintex K2 - process design, debugging, Smart Objects and deployment experience.
